FAQ

Common questions

What happens after I book a consultation? ↓
You'll receive a confirmation with a calendar invite. The call is 30 minutes — we'll listen, ask questions, and give you an honest assessment of how we can help.
How quickly can you start a project? ↓
For new projects, we typically start within 1–2 weeks of signing an agreement. For staff augmentation, we can often onboard team members within a week.
Do you work with startups or only enterprises? ↓
Both. We work with seed-stage startups building their first product, scale-ups modernizing their stack, and enterprise organizations undergoing digital transformation.
What time zones do you support? ↓
We have experience working across EMEA, APAC, and North American time zones. We've successfully delivered projects for clients in 15+ countries.
Do you offer fixed-price or time & materials contracts? ↓
Both. For well-defined projects, we offer fixed-price engagements. For evolving projects, we work on a time & materials basis. We'll recommend the right model during the consultation.
What makes Ramlatech different? ↓
Radical transparency, deep roots, and genuine care. We treat your challenges as our own. Our Palestinian heritage means we've built resilience, creativity, and a long-term view into everything we do.
