Named after one of Palestine's oldest cities, Ramlatech is a software agency with deep roots and a global mission — bridging heritage and innovation, one project at a time.

The Name

A name with 1,300 years of history behind it

Ramlatech is named after Ramla — one of Palestine's oldest cities, founded in 705 CE. Rich in history, architecture, and resilience, Ramla represents everything our company stands on: deep roots, enduring strength, and a commitment to legacy.

The Tech in our name is our compass — pointing toward digital transformation, modern software, and the power of innovation to reshape the world.

Together, Ramlatech embodies a bridge: between heritage and the future, between local identity and global impact. We believe that the most meaningful technology is built by people who know where they come from — and who build with purpose.
